Rejection Decoder

Paste a job rejection email. This reads it for two things: whether it's a mass-mailed template, and whether the wording looks AI-written. Nothing is uploaded — the analysis happens on your device.

Results will appear here once you analyze an email.
These are heuristic estimates, not proof. Email text alone can't conclusively show who or what wrote it — a person can send boilerplate, and a company can hand-write something that trips the AI flag. Weigh the scores alongside context like the sender address and response time.
